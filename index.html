Communities build communities

SPARK BUREAU is a not-for-profit community of thinkers, builders, movers, shakers, doers and believers that anyone can have an idea but no one can realise it alone.

coworking

Inspiring space to get stuff done and build your dream. Make our desk your own, float around, hire our boardroom or just drop in. However you choose to spend your time with us you will arrive impassioned and leave energised.

accelerator

We turn startups into scaleups by providing structured experiences for small cohorts of high-growth potential teams combining learning, mentoring, tools and coaching to prepare your business for follow-on investment.

education

Do you hear 'lean' and think of weight loss, 'agile' and think of gymnastics, 'design integration' and think of colour matching? Our national experts will help you correct assumptions, dispell myths and translate theory into practice.

investment

We appreciate that ideas require resources to bring to market - especially on a global scale. We'll help prepare you for the pitch and put you in front of both local angel investors and some of Australia's biggest VCs

All the knowledge, expertise and passion needed to solve the world's problems exists today but never in the same room at the same time speaking the same language and focused on a common purpose.
